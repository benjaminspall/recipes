Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of warm water
 - 2 tablespoons white sugar
 - 1 (.25 ounce) package bread machine yeast
 - 1/4 cup vegetable oil
 - 3 cups bread flour
 - 1 teaspoon of salt

Directions:
 1. Place the water, sugar and yeast in the pan of the bread machine. Let the
    yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes.

 2. Add the oil, flour and salt to the yeast. Select Basic or White Bread
    setting, and press Start.
